A little bit about me-

Hello to you! My name is Erin and I am a fullstack software engineer and web developer based in New York, NY.

I graduated from New York University in 2019 with a B.A. in Psychology. Now, how did I get into programming? During my third year of college, I realized I had some room to try different classes and decided to take Intro to Computer Programming. I did not have any prior experience with coding except for fidgiting around with my Myspace theme back in the day (ha ha). My interest in the class surprised me which lead me to take more computer science classes and take on a minor in Web Programming in Applications. In my final semster of college, I made the decision to switch my career path and pursue a career in software engineering. After graduating, I attended Fullstack Academy where my passion for programming was really solidified as I devloped new skills for my career. I continued to sharpen my skills as a Software Engineering Teaching Fellow at Fullstack while sharing my knowledge with the new incoming class.

Now that my time at Fullstack Academy has come to an end, I am currently on the hunt for my first developer role and am open to any full-time opportunities. I am excited to grow and add more skills to my tech toolbelt!

Recent Work

Bean

A crowd-sourced, user created tour guide progressive web app. Users can create, publish, or follow existing tours. When users follow tours, they are redirected to Google Maps where each location is mapped on a route. When users want to create tours, they can check in to locations near by or search for specific businesses.
Technologies used: Typescript, Ionic React, Firestore, Yelp API, Google Maps API

Stock Wallet

A full-stack JavaScript web-based stock portfolio app where users can buy stocks.Users can view their cash balance, stocks owned, and transaction history. Stock ticker symbols are dynamically colored based on their performance.
Technologies used: Javascript, Node.js, Express.js, React/React hooks, Redux, Sequelize, PostgreSQL, OAuth, Passport, CSS

E-Commerce-Commerce

A full-stack Javascript e-commerce web application for selling other websites. Users can add and delete items to their cart and update the quantity in the cart. Once users are done, they may complete their checkout using Stripe.
Technologies used: Javascript, Node.js, Express.js, React, Redux, Sequelize, PostgreSQL, OAuth, Passport, Semantic UI, CSS, Stripe

Nomification

A mobile app designed to notify users of their bookmarked locations when in close distance. Utilizing Yelp's API, users can bookmark businesses they want to visit at a later time.
Technologies used: JavaScript, React Native, Expo, Yelp API
